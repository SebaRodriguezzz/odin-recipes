Tortilla, Spanish Omelette, is a typical Spanish dish consisting of a thick egg omelette made with potatoes and fried in olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (6 per tortilla)
  • Potatoes (1kg per tortilla)
  • Onions (3 per tortilla)
  • Olive oil (enough to cover the potatoes when frying)
  • Salt (variable)

Steps

  1. Cook ingredients

    Heat oil in a pan over medium high heat, add potatoes and onions to cook, mixing the pan until the potato and onion are soft and not brown for 20 minutes.

  2. Beating eggs

    Beat 6 eggs into a large bowl until they turn a yellow pale colour, mix the onion and potatoes with a spoon to the beaten eggs and leave the oil in the pan.

  3. Add mixture

    Put another tbsp of oil into the pan with the other oil from the previous cook of onion and potato.

    Add the egg mixture, spreading the potatoes evenly in the pan in the egg mixture. Cook until the bottom of the tortilla is a light brown.

  4. Turn!

    Gently shake pan so the tortilla doesn't stick, then slide a spatula gently along the edges and underneath the tortilla. Place a large plate over pan and quickly turn plate and pan over so the tortilla will fall onto plate.

    Add 1 to the pan and slide the tortilla back in carefully tuck in sides with a fork, and continue cooking over medium heat until eggs are just set, about 3 minutes.

  5. Enjoy!
